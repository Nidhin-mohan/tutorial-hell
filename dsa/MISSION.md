# Mission: Data Structures & Algorithms for Coding Interviews

## Why
Land a role at a mid-size company or startup (target 15-20 LPA) within the next **3-6 months**. The user is a strong JavaScript developer (3 years) but has essentially **no DSA background** — interviews are the one gate standing between current skill and the next job, and DSA is the part they can't yet pass.

## Success looks like
- Confidently solve LeetCode **easy** and most **medium** problems in JavaScript within ~30 minutes.
- Recognise the common interview *pattern* a problem belongs to (hashing, two pointers, sliding window, BFS/DFS, etc.) and reach for it quickly.
- State the time and space complexity of a solution out loud and justify it.
- Talk through an approach clearly while coding — interview communication, not just a silent correct answer.

## Constraints
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months (revised 2026-07-15, up from an optimistic 1-2 months). The curriculum in `index.html` is 57 items at a floor of 3 sessions/week ≈ 19 weeks ≈ 4.4 months with no repeats — and repeats are expected, since a phase only closes when every problem is ✅. 3-6 months is the honest number; the old 1-2 was arithmetically impossible.
- **The floor is 3 sessions/week, not a daily grind.** Consistency over intensity — see the rules section in `index.html`. Never propose a plan that requires doubling up to catch up.
- **Starting point:** Knows JavaScript well; knows almost nothing about DSA. Lessons must build from true fundamentals and lean on existing JS fluency.
- **Difficulty bar:** Mid-size / startup — LeetCode easy-to-medium. No need for hard DP or competitive-programming tricks (yet).
- **Owns the Namaste DSA course** but finds it overwhelming (280+ FANG-level questions). Needs a *curated path through it*, not the firehose. We pick the right slice at the right time and skip out-of-scope FANG-only material.

## Out of scope (for now)
- LeetCode hard problems and contest-style tricks.
- Advanced DP, segment trees, union-find, advanced graph algorithms.
- The FANG-only portion of the Namaste DSA course.
- Language-specific trivia outside JavaScript.
